# Strategic Ecosystem Architecture: The Supply Chain

This document defines the technical and operational flow of value through the Moye Law ecosystem, utilizing the **Factory -> Builder -> Endpoint** pattern.

## 1. The Factory: Sushi Kitchen (The Source)
*   **Pattern Role:** `Factory Pattern` (Creates Objects/Systems)
*   **Function:** The raw engineering hub. It builds the underlying AI workflows, automation agents, and data pipelines.
*   **Output:** Unrefined "Bundles" (e.g., "Trust Monitoring System v1", "Change Detection Agent").
*   **Aesthetic:** Raw, Hacker, Neo-Brutalist, "Sushi" styled.

## 2. The Builder: Sushi Legal (The Packager)
*   **Pattern Role:** `Builder Pattern` (Assembles Complex Products)
*   **Function:** The regulatory and structuring layer. It takes the raw bundles from the Kitchen and "wraps" them in legal compliance, liability shielding, and user-friendly interfaces suitable for legal consumption.
*   **Output:** "Legal Products" ready for market (e.g., "WET Bot: Wills, Trusts & Estates Assistant").
*   **Aesthetic:** The Bridge. Technical but polished. "Refined Engineering".

## 3. The Endpoint: Moye Law (The Storefront)
*   **Pattern Role:** `Client / Endpoint` (Consumes & Presents)
*   **Function:** The delivery mechanism. It presents the finalized products to the client, either as standalone "Tech-Forward Solutions" (Retail) or as part of a "Bespoke Counsel" relationship (Private Banking).
*   **Output:** Client Services & Solutions.
*   **Aesthetic:** "Legal Luxury". Deep Navy, Gold, Serif. Trust & Authority.

## 📦 Archetype Example: WET Bot (Wills, Trusts & Estates Bot)
1.  **Kitchen Phase:** Engineers build a "Change Detection Agent" that monitors life events and a "Trust Database" schema.
2.  **Legal Phase:** Sushi Legal combines these into "WET Bot," adds legal disclaimers, configures the "Second Mind" logic for fiduciary duty, and packages it as a secure product.
3.  **Moye Law Phase:** The website displays "WET Bot" on the Estate Planning page as a premium tool for protecting your legacy, sold alongside expert counsel.

## 💡 Visual Implication
*   **Web Components:** Cards displaying these products (like WET Bot) should visually verify their "Sushi Legal" certification. They are "Powered by Sushi Kitchen" but "Certified by Sushi Legal."
