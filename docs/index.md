# The Moye Law Design System

## Philosophy: "Hybrid Mondrian"

The Moye Law digital identity is built on the intersection of three core concepts, designed to serve the firm's dual-persona clientele (High-Net-Worth Families and Tech Innovators).

### 1. The Grid is Justice (Mondrian)
We utilize a strict, neo-plasticist grid system inspired by **Piet Mondrian**.
*   **Structure:** All content is contained within 4px Deep Navy borders.
*   **Logic:** The grid represents the rigid framework of the law. It is immovable and absolute.
*   **Asymmetry:** We use the Golden Ratio (1:1.618) to create intentional imbalance, reflecting the dynamic nature of human relationships and innovation.

### 2. The Binary Lawyer (Dual Persona)
The brand identity acknowledges the two sides of Christopher Moye:
*   **The Artist/Lawyer (Serif):** Traditional, empathetic, warm. The "Counsel" side. Focused on Estate Planning and Family Law.
*   **The Engineer (Mono):** Technical, precise, efficient. The "Product" side. Focused on IP, AI, and Tech Law.

### 3. "Legal Luxury" (The Palette)
We reject standard "Corporate Blue." We embrace a palette that feels like a private club or a high-end atelier.
*   **Deep Navy (`#0A2342`):** The foundation. Replacing black.
*   **Warm Gold (`#C99D56`):** The value. Used for CTAs.
*   **Venetian Red (`#8B0000`):** The passion/art variation. Used sparingly.

## The "Storefront" vs "The Factory"
*   **The Storefront (Moye.law):** This is the client-facing consulting layer. It is polished, curated, and uses **Functional Minimalism**. It is safe for "Barbara Miller" (The Executor).
*   **The Factory (Sushi Kitchen):** This is the backend AI infrastructure. It is "Neo-Brutalist," "Hacker," and "Raw." **It strictly lives behind the curtain.** We do not expose the factory pipes to the storefront showroom.
